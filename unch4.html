Uncharted 4: A Thief's End

Synopsis:

While Nathan is 13 years old and is in the Catholic orphanage, his older brother Samuel, who has become a thief, makes him go to the wall to take him secretly to the house of a great archaeologist with belongings belonging to their mother who has was his colleague. Spotted by the old woman who alerts the police, the latter nevertheless recognizes them and agrees to leave them her notes before dying of a heart attack. The two brothers have to flee and understand that they have to change identities; remembering that their mother was persuaded that the legendary explorer Francis Drake had hidden heirs, they decide to adopt his name to start their new lives. Years later, Nathan and Sam, now adults, infiltrate a Panamanian prison alongside their ally Rafe Adler and are looking for information on pirate Henry Avery 's treasure . They discover that the latter has left a trail for any pirate who wanted to join his community to follow and that the latter leads to a church in Scotland. In their escape, Rafe kills a guard and Sam gets shot. Presumed dead, Nathan decides to drop the case, unlike Rafe.
