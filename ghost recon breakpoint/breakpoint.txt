In 2023, political murders propagated by drones from billionaire Jace Skell's firm are on the rise. After all contact is lost with Auroa, the fictional island home to Skell Technology's headquarters, the Ghosts team is sent to the island.

This simple player-driven reconnaissance mission turns into a true survival mission 1 when the unit is stormed by the Wolves, former Ghosts led by Cole D. Walker who have taken over the island .
